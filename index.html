About Me:

I am 31 years old, born and raised from Blooming Grove, New York. During and a little after high school I had a bit of odd jobs being a camp counselor, lifeguard and working at Godiva Chocolate. I have been in the Marine Corps for almost 12 years (joined November 2012) and I am now going to school to become a commissioned officer. My first job was as a Military Police. I have been stationed in several places including:

  • Quantico, Virginia with Presidential Helicopter Squadron.
  • Okinawa, Japan with 3rd Law Enforcement Battalion
  • Marine Corps Recruit Depot Parris Island, South Caralina as a Drill Instructor
  • University of Colorado-Boulder NROTC as a student

I have a 4 year old mut, Pebbles. I've had her for about two and half years now and she is basically my shadow. I enjoy doing anything and everything outdoors. That includes, paddle boarding, kayaking, hiking, backpacking, biking. One of my biggest passions is to travel. So far, not counting the 3 countries I plan on visiting in 2025, I have been to 22 countries. I am only missing South America and Antartica to have travelled to every continent. South America is getting crossed off January 2025. I have very little experience with website design but excited to learn!
