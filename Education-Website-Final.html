Overview

Universal Design shouldn't be an afterthought but the standard to all designs from the beginning. When everyone is thought of no matter the disability, the website is open to as many people as possible. This shouldn't be a suggestion but more of the standard to Web Design. This article will dive into the What, How and Why towards Universal Design.

Conclusion

Universal Design is the best thing to attract the most amount of people to your website and allowing them to have a pleasant experience. This design isn't targeted at just people with disabilities, but allows the website to be inclusive to everyone without isolating a specific type of user. This does not make websites boring but it does allow for a better search engine optimization and is easier to adjust overtime. Universal Design ensures inclusion, while also keeping it visually appealing and helps everyone aquire different knowledge.
